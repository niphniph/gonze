F.A.Q.

Is it beginner-friendly? expand_more
Absolutely. We have fitness assistants on the floor to help you with any equipment. You set your own pace.
Can I just come for the music? expand_more
Yes! We have chill zones and viewing areas. You don't have to lift a single weight to enjoy the GYM WAVE energy.
What is the dress code? expand_more
Comfortable sportswear or stylish athleisure. Neon accents are encouraged. Clean indoor training shoes are mandatory.
How safe is the equipment? expand_more
We use only professional-grade equipment. Certified trainers roam the floor to ensure safety and proper form.
