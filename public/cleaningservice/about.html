The Vision of Thiago Goff

Aya Cleaning Solutions was conceived not as a cleaning company, but as an environmental restoration studio.

Thiago realized that modern, high-end estates require deep specialized material knowledge. Standard chemical sprays strip the natural luster of custom marbles, damage fine hardwood finishes, and dry out delicate antiques. Aya was created to restore these items to their pristine original form, providing meticulous luxury for homeowners who accept nothing less than visual perfection.

Under Thiago's strict guidance, every crew member undergoes intensive training in safe material detailing, dust-containment protocol, and client confidentiality. This creates a lasting bond of Fresh Trust that our clients value above all else.

The Aya Restorative Protocol

Our rigorous, three-phase clinical detailing system engineered for absolute material safety and pristine results.

01. Dust Extraction

We deploy high-filtration air and wall extraction systems to extract up to 99.9% of microscopic fine particulates, airborne allergens, and surface dust without kicking them into the atmosphere.

02. Material Detailing

We analyze every surface and apply matching botanical formulas. From premium cream velvet sofas to polished granite counter trim and high-gloss gold fixture finishes, we detail every inch safely.

03. Organic Shielding

The final phase applies eco-premium coatings to protect custom woods, seal marble grouting, and distribute an extremely subtle custom organic scent to solidify sensory calm.
