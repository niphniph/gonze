Awakening the Self

The Georgian Institute of Psychosynthesis is dedicated to the profound exploration of human potential. We weave together rigorous academic research, clinical practice, and a deep reverence for the organic nature of psychological growth.

Our Ethos

psychology

Integrative Healing

We view the psyche not merely as a mechanism to be repaired, but as a dynamic, living ecosystem. Our approach seeks to synthesize disparate elements of personality into a cohesive, transcendent whole, fostering true autonomy and deep connection.

history_edu

Academic Rigor

Rooted in classical traditions yet embracing modern clinical methodologies, our research archives provide an unparalleled resource for serious students of depth psychology.
